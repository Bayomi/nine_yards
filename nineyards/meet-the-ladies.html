The Ladies Behind the Scenes

Pragya is the main person of contact between 9Y and Atijeevan Foundation. She was attacked in 2006, on a train when she was traveling to Delhi for her campus placement interview, twelve days after her marriage. Her culprit was a jilted man, ten years older to her, whose marriage proposal she had refused. Since her attack Pragya has undergone a total of nine surgeries, as well as two severe miscarriages. However, she was later able to conceive four year old Aishi - "I forgot all my pain and miseries when I held Aishi in my hands. And after that there was no turning back, I have devoted my energy into motivating other acid attack survivors to get up and keep going."

Pragya

Rupa was attacked in 2008 when she was in 7th grade. She was attacked by her step-mother, who didn't want Rupa to get married, as she was her primary assistant in doing household chores. Rupa did not receive immediate medical treatment after her attack and accordingly has undergone 11 reconstructive surgeries. Although the perpetrator was put behind bars, she was released on the premise that the village would accumulate a monetary sum and pay this amount to Rupa. However, Rupa never received this financial aid. Shaina was attacked in 2009 by a man who was pursuing her for marriage and when she refused, he threw acid on her face in an open market in Delhi. The man had been followingShaina around for 3 to 4 months, until a fight struck between the two, and the man threw acid on her face. Shaina has a family of six members relying on her as the breadwinner - she is responsible for the education of all of her four younger siblings. Currently, the whole family works as tailors, however, they struggle with income relative to their large family.
